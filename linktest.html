    Features

    Navigation

    Jeeves is equipped with Mecanuum wheels and is programmed to drive to select locations of PSU's 4th Ave building lower level.

    Kinect

    Jeeves has Kinect RGB-D camera, connected via USB 2.0 to the main computer. The Kinect is useful for human-robot Interaction and obstacle detection.
